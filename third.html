A Introduction to the new Election Preview Team

A major facet of Election Preview, is its ability to provide the community with accurate information surrounding Elections. As we expand ourselves into this field, first we’d like you to meet the Election Preview Team.

Colin Leary

Hi, my name is Colin Leary. I’ve always strived to attain a deeper level of understanding surrounding human geography. Understanding Political boundaries, and what causes them, is undoubtedly at the forefront of this endeavor. Ever since I was old enough to understand elections, I’ve harbored a disdain for the mentality of alienating political opponents. Instead, I believe in taking a closer look at what socio-economic and geographic factors have carved out this divide in our country’s politics. I’ve adopted mapping software as a tool to understand why people think and act in the ways they do. We need not homogenize our beliefs, but allow ourselves to understand how similar we all truly are. I believe information and data mapping is the most effective method for achieving this, and achieving a stronger, more determined nation.

Noah Warren

Hi, my name is Noah Warren. Throughout my life I’ve been fascinated by politics and government. At the age of 5, I became obsessed with knowing everything about US Presidents. I even took it upon myself to memorize all 43 concurrent presidents. My fascination continued into COVID where I filled notebooks out with details about each Presidential Election, and major pieces of legislation passed during their term. Since 2017, I’ve worked alongside the Vice Mayor of Alexandria, providing a deeper understanding of our political system. It was then that I grew to love politics and government. At Election Preview, I want to display this passion to the world.
